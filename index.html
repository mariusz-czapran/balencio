Balencio: Bank Balance Sim

Wealth and crypto portfolio simulator.

Ever wanted to feel like a millionaire? Use Balencio to show your friends a custom bank balance for entertainment. Balencio is a wealth and crypto portfolio simulator designed for mobile devices.

Key Features

Custom Balance Set any amount in your main account to show off in seconds.
Crypto Portfolio Simulate holdings like Bitcoin, Ethereum, and more in a modern interface.
Schedule Income Set a timer to receive a simulated deposit notification on your device.
Minimalist Design Dark and light mode options inspired by high-end banking interfaces.
